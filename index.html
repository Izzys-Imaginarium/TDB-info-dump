Darkwick Academy is an educational facility managed by the Anomalous Investigation Institute. Darkwick Academy is also referred to as Darkwick. It is a renowned private academy, which has held the number-one ranking nationally for decades. Darkwick is located on an artificial island in Tokyo Bay and entry by outsiders is strictly prohibited. The number of applicants who pass their top-secret Darkwick entrance exam is a closely guarded secret, and it is rumored to be extremely competitive. Darkwick is famous for producing elites who play key roles on both the national and world stage. Darkwick has a gothic-looking main building, grounds that stretch for miles and is surrounded by thick woods. Darkwick has stone paths are lined with colorful flower beds along the lush grass. A glittering fountain stands in the center. The island is larger than it appears from the mainland, offering a unique and peaceful atmosphere. Darkwick has many cats in hats emblazoned with the Darkwick insignia darting all around, with relaxed expressions and glossy coats. The many cats at Darkwick act as messengers, maintenance staff, and cleaning staff. The main Darkwick Academy building features a spacious hallway adorned with marble pillars, extending beneath an intricately decorated ceiling and a patterned carpet. The counseling room is a comfortable lounge with warm wooden walls and a fireplace in the center. The chancellor's office features a grand spiral staircase and an extravagant chandelier, creating an atmosphere of grandeur that deters unfamiliar visitors. Students travel to and from Darkwick Academy on the Galaxy Express. The Galaxy Express travels on the catsmo lines. Students use a smartphone app called "catsmo" to enter, exit, and travel on the Galaxy Express. In the Galaxy Express, there is "lost property" floating high in the air. The Galaxy Express train itself is a steam train with soft velvet seats and an old-fashioned vibe, he walls are wooden with antique lamps hanging from the ceiling, and the starry sky is visible from the windows. The conductor of the Galaxy Express is a cat. Currently Darkwick Academy has 7 houses: Frostheim, Vegastrom, Jabberwock, Sinostra, Hotarubi, Obscuary, and Mortkranken. Darkwick used to be 3 other house, but they have been closed following the Clash. The closed Darkwick houses were called Dionysia, Ultio, and Clementia. The Anomalous Investigation Institute funds and oversees Darkwick Academy. The Anomalous Investigation Institute has search and recovery personnel to help investigate cases and incidents after they conclude by gathering and cleaning up evidence. When a student is admitted to Darkwick they are assigned to their dormatory using during a ceremony where a ritual called the "weighing of souls" is performed. Darkwick academy offers a variety of classes including: Anomalous Medicine, Anomalous Combat, Anomalous Biology, Anomalous Law, and Anomalous Information Studies. Darkwick students message and communicate through a cell phone app called WickChat. Darkwick reports important information through talking portraits, the system is called Masterpiece News. All the dorms are competing to win the Laurel Crown. Students at Darkwick take on missions for their houses. General students take on low ranking missions while ghouls take on more difficult or dangerous missions. The goal of most missions is to investigate anomalous events and in some cases to subdue, kill, and/or capture anomalies for research purposes.
